01
The 77th Director
第七十七代堂主

Hu Tao is the 77th director of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor — a position she inherited at a remarkably young age. Despite the somber nature of her work, she approaches every funeral with a unique blend of solemn respect and lively enthusiasm, believing that death is merely another chapter in the grand story of existence.

As a poet at heart, she composes elegiac couplets for each soul she escorts to the afterlife, finding beauty in the spaces between life and death.

02
A Heart of Mischief
淘气之心

Behind her professional facade lies an irrepressible prankster. Hu Tao delights in startling her friends with sudden appearances, composing cheeky limericks about her clients, and engaging in playful banter with anyone who crosses her path.

Her nickname "Tao" (meaning "naughty" in Chinese) suits her perfectly — she treats life as an endless game and invites everyone to play along, even as she prepares them for their final journey.

"The butterfly dances on the edge of a blade,
Unaware that the wind has already made its decision.
Yet still it dances, still it plays —
For that is the only way to live."
Plum blossoms bloom even in the coldest winter.
03
Pyro · Spirit of Embers
火元素 · 燃愿之心

As a Pyro vision bearer, Hu Tao wields flames that burn with a deep crimson hue — not the fire of destruction, but the warmth of remembrance. Her flame butterflies, called "Spirit of Embers," carry the souls of the departed to their final resting place.

In battle, she dances with her signature Staff of Homa, a polearm that channels both her Pyro power and her unyielding spirit. Each strike is accompanied by butterfly-shaped flames and the faint echo of funeral bells.

✦ Abilities

Combat & Skills

Hu Tao's unique combat style blends fire, finesse, and the supernatural.

🦋
Guide to Afterlife
Enter the Paramita Papilio state, unleashing Pyro-infused attacks that scale with max HP. Butterfly spirits guide each strike.
🌺
Blood Blossom
Enemies struck by Hu Tao's charged attacks are marked with Blood Blossoms — flowers that bloom with Pyro damage over time.
⚰️
Spirit Soother
Unleash a devastating Pyro AoE burst that deals massive damage and regenerates HP for Hu Tao based on the number of enemies hit.
📜
Elegy for the Fallen
When Hu Tao falls below 30% HP or is defeated, she gains a significant Pyro DMG bonus — a final, fiery blessing.
🔥
Flame Integration
Hu Tao's charged attacks consume stamina to perform a lunge that applies Blood Blossom. Each crit rate increases party CRIT except herself.
👻
The Ghostly Guide
Hu Tao's passive talent increases critical rate for all party members (except Hu Tao) by 12% for 10s after using Spirit Soother.
