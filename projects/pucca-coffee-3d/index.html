An Interactive Narrative

The Legend of Sooga Village Coffee

Scroll through the chapters to witness how Pucca transformed her three uncles' noodle empire into the world's most electrifying hot and iced coffee destination.

1
Chapter I • The Delivery Girl

Supersonic Scooter & Jajang Noodles

Pucca lives at the legendary Goh-Rong Restaurant with her three devoted uncles: Uncle Dumpling, Ho, and Linguini. Known across Sooga Village for her red long-sleeved shirt, odango double buns, and boundless superhuman strength, she delivers piping hot bowls of Jajang Noodles on her trusty red scooter. But when townspeople began craving an afternoon boost that could keep up with her energy, a new idea was born.

"When Pucca pushes the Leaning Tower of Pisa or runs across the Pacific Ocean, ordinary tea just isn't enough. We needed a brew with superhuman heart!" — Chef Ho
Goh-Rong Flagship Pair
2
Chapter II • The Silent Crush

Garu's Vow of Silence & Midnight Espresso

Enter Garu, the stoic 12-year-old ninja-in-training who has taken a sacred vow of silence to restore his family's honor. Constantly leaping across bamboo rooftops to evade Pucca's loving ambush of hugs and kisses, Garu needed a high-octane fuel to stay alert. Pucca personally formulated the Midnight Shuriken Double Espresso—dark roasted, velvety, and served in a matte-black ceramic cup.

*Garu sips espresso, blinks rapidly with intense focus, and silently leaps 50 feet into the air to dodge Pucca's incoming flying hug.*
Garu's Dark Shuriken Brew
3
Chapter III • Culinary Mastery

The Secret of the Three Chefs

Uncle Dumpling, Ho, and Linguini didn't just brew ordinary coffee; they applied centuries of culinary technique. They infused high-altitude roasted arabica beans with traditional sweet red bean (azuki) syrup, butterscotch silk caramel, and frothy oat milk. The result? A menu split perfectly between Steaming Hot Silk Lattes for cozy rainy evenings and Crystal Iced Nitro Brews for high-speed summer adventures.

"We treat coffee beans with the exact same reverence and fire as our hand-pulled noodles!" — Uncle Dumpling
Uncle Dumpling's Caramel Silk
4
Chapter IV • The Village Challenge

Ching's Matcha vs Tobe's Fiery Chili Mocha

As the coffee menu expanded, Pucca's best friend Ching (often accompanied by her pet chicken Won on her head) crafted the tranquil layered Matcha Espresso Iced Latte. Meanwhile, Garu's rival, the scarred ninja Tobe, attempted to outspice the Goh-Rong kitchen with his explosive Fiery Chili Chocolate Mocha. Despite their rivalry, every cup is now served exclusively through Pucca's delivery network.

"Even Tobe pauses his ninja vengeance for a 3 PM iced cold brew break!" — Abyo, Kung-Fu Action Enthusiast
