01 / The Craft
Design Philosophy

Born in the funeral parlor, dressed for the finish line.

Every panel of the GT-77 is a negotiation between two worlds — the Director insisted the aerodynamics should “let the butterflies do the heavy lifting.” Engineering agreed.

Twin morphing wing-planes unfold like papilio wings under braking, bleeding speed the way spirit flames bleed into the night — silently, beautifully, and all at once.
